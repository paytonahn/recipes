How to Make Blueberry Pie

Ingredients

Pastry
  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2/3 cup shortening
  • 4 tablespoons cold water
    • Filling
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon (optional)
  • 6 cups blueburries
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon butter (optional)

    • Step-by-Step

    1. Set an oven rack and preheat to 375 F.

    2. Mix sugar, cornstarch, cinnamon, and salt together; sprinkle over blueberries.

    3. Line pie dish with pie crust. Pour mix into crust and dot with butter. Cut remaining pastry into strips. Crimp and flute edges.

    4. Bake pie till filling bubbles, which typically takes around 50 minutes.
