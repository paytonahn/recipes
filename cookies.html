How to Make Cookies

Ingredients
  • 1/4 cup flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup butter
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 2 cups choclate chips
  • cup nuts (optional)
    • Step-by-Step
    1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F; Mix flour, baking soda, and salt.
    2. Mix softened butter and sugar with mixer for a minute till fluffy consistency.
    3. Beat in egg and vanilla till smooth. Stir in flour mixture and stir till blended. Then add chips and nuts.
    4. Round up dough and roll into balls. Place dough balls on cookie sheet evenly spaced apart.
    5. Bake for 10 minutes till light brown and let it sit for thirty minutes.
