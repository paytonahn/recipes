How to Make Brownies

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/3 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking powder
    • Step-by-Step

    1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F; Grease and flour your pan.

    2. Melt 1/2 cup butter in a saucepan. Remove from heat, stir in a mix of sugar, eggs, and 1 teaspoon of vanilla. Beat in 1/3 cup cocoa, 1/2 cup flour, salt, and baking powder. Spread batter into prepared pan.

    3. Bake in oven for 30 minutes.
